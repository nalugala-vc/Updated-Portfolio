RECHAUFFE FOODS

Designing a food waste management application that generates personalized recipes from leftovers

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Food wastage is a rampant issue currently. This is caused by busy schedules leading to poor meal planning, inefficient use of ingredients, and poor eating habits. The reasons outlined above eventually lead to a lot of food being leftover, and in the end, disposed of.

GOAL

To design a mobile application that uses Recommender systems to help users come up with meals to cook using leftover ingredients.

Research

Research Goal

The aimed to delve into the current behaviors, attitudes, and challenges surrounding food waste, meal planning, and the utilization of leftovers. I was particularly interested in identifying user preferences and expectations for a mobile application designed to address these issues. The primary focus was on creating an understanding of how individuals manage food waste and exploring their needs when it comes to personalized recipes generated from leftover ingredients.

Asking the right Questions

Understanding User Behavior

  • What are the common reasons for food wastage among individuals?
  • How frequently do users find themselves with leftover ingredients after meals?
  • What are the main challenges users face in meal planning and utilizing leftovers?

    • Awareness and Knowledge:

  • How aware are users of the environmental impact of food wastage?
  • What knowledge do users have about the nutritional value and safety of using leftover ingredients?

    • Current Solutions and Habits

  • Are users currently using any apps or methods to manage food waste and plan meals?
  • What are the existing habits and practices regarding meal planning and utilizing leftovers?

    • Preferences for Recipe Recommendations:

  • What type of recipes are users generally interested in when using leftover ingredients?
  • Do users prefer quick and easy recipes or are they willing to invest more time in cooking?

    • User Interface and Experience Preferences:

  • What features would users expect in a food waste management app?
  • What kind of user interface and navigation would enhance the user experience?

    • Feedback and Improvement:

  • How willing are users to provide feedback on the recipes suggested by the app?
  • What improvements would users like to see in the app over time?

    • The audience

  • Busy professionals and working parents - individuals with limited time for meal planning hence they use the application to save on time.
  • People living on a tight budget - These people have limited resources hence this application would help them make most use of leftovers promoting affordable meals .
  • Food enthusiasts - This are people interested in exploring new recipes.
  • People who are picky eaters (e.g parents with young children) - This application offers various meal suggestions to meet the ever changing taste buds needs of the user by offering various alternatives.

    • Persona Details

    As part of processing the research findings, I analyze the information that found on the research phase by creating a User Persona. This persona will contain the details of user’s goals, needs, frustrations, interests, and also other information that might useful in the design process such as user’s profile, personality and behavioral traits.

    Proposed Solution

    Functional Requirements

    The app should enable users to

  • Create accounts and manage their profiles
  • Input their leftover and generate recipes based on the leftover ingredients
  • Display well detailed recipes including the nutritional information and step by step cooking instructions of and number of servings of the suggested recipes
  • Save recipes to their profile
  • Rate and review recipes
  • Provide users with information on how to preserve the suggested meals

    • Non Functional Requirements

  • Security of user data i.e data backup, secure authentication.
  • Performance; should respond quickly to user actions i.e generate recipe suggestions fast and effectively handle a large number of users.
  • Scalability: Should be able to smoothly handle large number of users. Have friendly user interfaces: users should be able to navigate through the application with ease.

    • Design Considerations

    "Know your population"

    For busy professionals

  • Provide options to filter or sort recipes, offer a quick recipe feature and a favourites section

    • For people living on a tight budget

  • Offer suggestions on how to repurpose leftovers into new meals and include a budget-friendly additions feature that suggests low-cost ingredients.

    • For food enthusiasts

  • Allow users to customize the recipe generation process by specifying their preferred cuisines or flavors and offer a "Chef's Inspiration" section that showcases innovative and creative ways to transform leftovers.

    • For picky eaters

  • Offer recipe alternatives and variations that can be created using the entered leftovers, include a feature to customize and adjust the flavour profiles and provide an option to exclude specific ingredients or allergens from the recipe suggestions.
